How to Participate


if you stand on a place, and feel all parts of the world under your feet...

1. Take your soil

One fist of your soil. The soil in which you implant your memories, dreams and hopes. The soild that you feel attached to.

2. Send it to me

Put your soil in a plastic bag and send it to this address.

Address Kunstuniversität Linz
Raum & Design Strategien
Reindlstr. 16 – 18
4040 Linz

3. Join on the map

Take a photo or video from your hand with the soil and please answer this question: If you stand on a place, and feel all parts of the world under your feet, what would you do? What would you like to say? What would be your wish?

  • Upload documents

4. And if...

If ten of you send me your soil, I promise to carry them with my bike and start everyone’s land from 1 m. x 1 m.
If hundereds of you send me your soil, I promise to carry them in the back of a car and start everyone’s land from 10 m. x 10 m. 400,000 participant’s soil would cover 201 m² plaza. This is the minimum portion of soils we need to cover first tile.
Fortunateley I have whole sopport from Linz Art University to make the last phase of project real. whatever the portion of soils would be, I build the plaza, even if the soils cover 1m², it would be enough for standing just one person on it, feel the washed soils under the feet and start to talk ...
If millions of you send me your soil...,
how far can we go together?
