Public plaza for comunication

plaza will be a public scene for artists and people who want to interact directly with other people

My project is a public plaza. For creating this plaza, I am asking you around the world to send me one fist of soil. Metaphorically, each of the contributors would authorize me to build the plaza; each fist of soil signifies this authorization from another inhabitant of this earth. You can send me the soil that you feel most attached to. Each portion will represent a specific identity, culture, story and myth. In spite of all that, All soils are be mixed on the plaza and be given a completely new identity. They will be washed from all differences and contrasts. They are not bear the past anymore. The future will determine their identity.

It is no one’s land, and everyone’s land. The soils make a new land which belongs to everyone.
The land which is be built based on unanimity and empathy. The place of that plaza is in the public area in the city. The design of the plaza is based on the function of a public place. The plaza will be a public scene for artists and people who want to interact directly with other people.

Area

The area of the plaza is the result of division of the total area of lands to the current population of the earth.
I have divided this area to 100 tiles. The area of this plaza is 201 m². In fact, it is only one portion of all that one person is entitled to.
